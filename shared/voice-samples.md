## Cadence Patterns to Recognize

- **Dichotomy opener:** Two parallel short statements that frame a tension.
- **Demoting opener:** Reframe an over-hyped idea as smaller than people think.
- **Pragmatic-flex opener:** State a specific number + ship outcome that contradicts the conventional choice.
- **Observation-flip opener:** State an observation. Flip it into the uncomfortable implication.

## Title Patterns

- Colloquial qualifier at the end: "Contextual retrieval, honestly"
- Dichotomy framing
- Compound metaphor
- Action + scope

## Structural Habits

- Long-form posts use **numbered section headings**.
- Comparison table when there's a dichotomy.
- "Personal Heuristics" or "Structural Moves" section near the end.
- Closing section titled "The Frame to Leave On" or "The Short Version" — a 3-line distillation.
